Welcome to PsyMed!

The WebApp is a tool designed for mental health professionals that facilitates appointment management, data recording, and tracking of patients' clinical history. With an intuitive system, it allows organizing all relevant information in one place, enhancing efficiency and ensuring personalized and secure care.

¿Quienes somos?

PsyMed es una startup formada por un grupo de universitarios comprometidos con la creación de una app que facilita la administración de la salud mental. Nuestra misión es apoyar a los médicos en la gestión eficiente de citas, el registro de datos, y el seguimiento del historial clínico de sus pacientes, todo en una plataforma intuitiva y segura. Buscamos mejorar la calidad de la atención y optimizar el trabajo de los profesionales de la salud mental.

What is it about?

It's a web platform aimed at the mental health field. We provide users with the necessary tools to efficiently manage medical appointments, treatments, and patient status, which can be recorded with the data the attending physician must use. This platform helps psychiatrists by monitoring patients in real time, seeking to reduce the required usage time and providing a quality solution.

Benefits & Value Proposition

Appointment Control

Easily manage, schedule, and keep track of patient appointments with an intuitive calendar interface.

Data Registration

Securely record and update patient data, from basic information to detailed clinical histories.

Medical Prescriptions

Generate and store digital prescriptions that can be accessed by patients and professionals anytime.

Online Monitoring

Track patient progress remotely using live metrics and updates shared through the platform.

Creation of Medical Profiles

Build and maintain comprehensive medical profiles for each patient to improve diagnosis and treatment accuracy.

Centralized Medical History

Have all patient history and progress in one place for accurate, informed care decisions.

Advantages

  • Secure and confidential data handling
  • User-friendly interface for professionals
  • Designed specifically for psychiatric care environments
  • Supports better clinical decision-making
  • Real-time patient progress tracking
  • Integrated appointment and medication scheduling
  • Centralized medical records for quick access
  • Easy-to-use mobile and web access for patients and professionals
