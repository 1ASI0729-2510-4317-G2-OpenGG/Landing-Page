What is it about?

It's a web platform aimed at the mental health field. We provide users with the necessary tools to efficiently manage medical appointments, treatments, and patient status, which can be recorded with the data the attending physician must use. This platform helps psychiatrists by monitoring patients in real time, seeking to reduce the required usage time and providing a quality solution.
